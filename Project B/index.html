WAR IS HELL

An interactive story of how peace can quickly turn into chaos

TRIGGER WARNING: User discretion advised

The world and its inhabitant (us humans) has not always been peaceful. In our short history there are countless instances of peace and chaos. Today can be a peaceful day in the city where everything is silent, but at the very next hour or even minute everything could change
